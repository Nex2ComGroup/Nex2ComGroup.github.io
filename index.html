Nex2Com Group

Next-generation Communication and Computation Optimization

描述 (Description)

中文:
Nex2Com小组是广西大学的一支研究团队，由张教授领导，致力于大规模分布式计算和通信优化，重点研究大语言模型（LLM）优化与应用。我们的研究涉及多个前沿领域，包括分布式系统、计算优化、通信网络和人工智能。当前研究方向包括：基于网络资源的联邦学习架构优化、资源受限设备上的分布式模型训练、在线自适应模型、通信与计算的协同优化、新型去中心化和分布式训练范式、LLM在通信领域的应用、以及数字人技术等。

English:
The "Nex2Com" Research Group is a team at Guangxi University, led by Prof. Zhenrong Zhang, focused on large-scale distributed computing and communication optimization, particularly on optimizing and applying large language models (LLMs). Our research spans several cutting-edge areas, including distributed systems, computation optimization, communication networks, and artificial intelligence. We are currently working on several projects, with key research directions including optimization of federated learning architectures based on network resources, distributed model training on resource-constrained devices, online adaptive models, collaborative optimization of communication and computation, novel decentralized and distributed training paradigms, LLM applications in communications, and digital human technologies.

当前研究方向 (Current Research Directions)

中文:

  • 基于大语言模型的智能体设计
  • 联邦大模型中的高效通信与计算优化
  • 基于资源受限设备的分布式模型训练与推理
  • 强化学习在通信优化中的应用
  • 基于强化学习的联邦学习
  • 基于区块链的去中心化联邦学习优化

English:

  • Agent Design Based on Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Efficient Communication and Computing Optimization Among Federated Large Models
  • Distributed Model Training and Inference Over Resource-Constrained Devices
  • Reinforcement Learning for Communication Optimization
  • Federated Learning Based on Reinforcement Learning
  • Blockchain-Based Decentralized Federated Learning Optimization

English:
