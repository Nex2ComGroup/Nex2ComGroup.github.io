Description

The "Nex2Com" Research Group is a team at Guangxi University, led by Prof. Zhenrong Zhang, focused on large-scale distributed computing and communication optimization, particularly on optimizing and applying large language models (LLMs). Our research spans several cutting-edge areas, including distributed systems, computation optimization, communication networks, and artificial intelligence.

Current Research Directions

  1. Agent Design Based on Large Language Models (LLMs)
  2. Efficient Communication and Computing Optimization Among Federated Large Models
  3. Distributed Model Training and Inference Over Resource-Constrained Devices
  4. Reinforcement Learning for Communication Optimization
  5. Federated Learning Based on Reinforcement Learning
  6. Blockchain-Based Decentralized Federated Learning Optimization

Open Invitation

We warmly welcome undergraduate and graduate students to join our research group and participate in these cutting-edge research topics. If you are interested in other areas of research not covered in our current focus, feel free to contact @fangz for further discussion.

简介

Nex2Com 小组是广西大学的一支研究团队，由张教授领导，致力于大规模分布式计算和通信优化，重点研究大语言模型（LLM）优化与应用。我们的研究涉及多个前沿领域，包括分布式系统、计算优化、通信网络和人工智能。

当前研究方向

  1. 基于大语言模型的智能体设计
  2. 联邦大模型中的高效通信与计算优化
  3. 基于资源受限设备的分布式模型训练与推理
  4. 强化学习在通信优化中的应用
  5. 基于强化学习的联邦学习
  6. 基于区块链的去中心化联邦学习优化

我们诚邀本科生和研究生加入我们的研究小组，参与这些前沿课题的研究。如果您对其他研究领域感兴趣，且该领域尚未包含在我们的研究方向中，欢迎联系@fangz进行详细沟通。
